About

Dual perspectives are essential to High C. We are a designer and a manufacturer, with roots in Ukraine. In 2018, after nearly a decade spent developing fabrics and producing apparel for the Ukraine market, we opened a factory in Lviv, where we make our collections to this day.

Our designs balance a modern perspective natural to the Pacific Northwest with an ingrained value of technique, discipline and detail.

We combine premium materials with a forward-thinking focus on simplicity and precision, and aim to elevate classic design through a mastery of process.

Service

While we try to respond to all inquiries on the same day, some requests may require up to 48 hours.

Tracking Your Order

Once a purchase has been completed, you will receive a confirmation email outlining the details of your order. After the order has been processed and shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email, which will include tracking details.

Item Availability

high c. ensures that stock availability online matches our inventory on hand. If for some reason an item purchased is not in stock, you will receive an email notification immediately and your order will be refunded.

Order Cancellation

high c. reserves the right to cancel any orders suspected of fraudulent activity. Any orders cancelled as a result of this will be notified by email, and will be refunded in full. To avoid order cancellation, we suggest purchasing with PayPal where additional screening is already in place.
